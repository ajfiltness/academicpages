SafetyCube

Ashleigh Filtness
Jul 5, 2018 project
Project Website Press Release Transport Safety

SafetyCube (Safety CaUsation, Benefits and Efficiency) was a research project funded by the European Commission under Horizon 2020, the objective of SafetyCube was to develop an innovative road safety Decision Support System (DSS) which enables policy-makers and stakeholders to select and implement the most appropriate strategies, measures and cost-effective approaches to reduce all road user casualties. The significance of this work was recognised by a Prince Michael International Road Safety Award in 2019
